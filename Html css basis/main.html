Philosophy of existence


It is an elucidation of one's attitude to living life. Simply speaking, it explains a person's belief as to the way in which one should live life or utilize it. Nothing gives an individual a sense of purpose in the world like having a clear-cut philosophy of life

The philosophy of existence contains two major componets

Importance of life
Importance of ethics
